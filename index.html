David R. Cok - Consulting and scientific activities

Software safety and security: I improve software safety and security -- ensuring that software does what it is supposed to (safety) and does not allow what it is not supposed to (security) --
  • by working with clients to review and improve their software
  • and my own research and publication in software correctness.

Current Professional Activities

  • Consulting to improve or validate software (2017 - present)
  • Visiting scientist at CEA, contributing to the ACSL++ extension of ACSL to C++ and the frama-clang plugin to Frama-C to support ACSL++
  • Significant contributor to JML (the Java Modeling Language) for writing behavioral specifications for Java
  • Principal developer of the OpenJML to verify Java programs against JML specifications.
  • Contributor to the ACSL specification language for C programs
  • Contributor to GrammaTech (2010 - 2017)
  • Senior research scientist and research manager at Kodak Research Lab (1982 - 2010)
  • Postdoc at Argonne National Lab (1980 - 1982)
  • Ph.D. (1980) (and Masters - 1977) in Physics from Harverd University
  • A.B. degrees in Mathematics and in Physics, Calvin College (1975)
